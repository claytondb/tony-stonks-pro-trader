# Tony Stonks Pro Trader
## UX/UI Design Specification

---

## 1. Design Philosophy

**Core Principles:**
- **Instant Readability:** Player must always know score, combo, time, and objectives at a glance
- **Minimal Intrusion:** HUD should be visible but not distract from gameplay
- **Corporate Satire:** UI mimics corporate software, spreadsheets, and office aesthetics... but cooler
- **Responsive:** Works on 4K desktop, mobile portrait, and everything between

**Visual Theme:** "Excel Meets Extreme Sports"
- Clean, grid-based layouts
- Spreadsheet/form aesthetic for menus
- Neon accent colors pop against neutral office grays
- Motion graphics use sliding, tabular transitions

### HUD Elements

**Timer (Top Left)**
- Digital clock aesthetic
- Flashes red under 30 seconds
- "OVERTIME" effect when time runs out (slow-mo landing)

**Score (Top Right)**
- Large, always visible
- Score flies in from trick popup
- Pulses when increasing

**Objectives (Left Side)**
- Collapsible panel
- Checkmarks animate when completed
- Current objective highlighted
- Press TAB to expand full list

**Trick Popup (Center)**
- Appears during combos
- Shows: Trick name + Base points Ã— Multiplier
- Stacks for multi-trick combos
- Fades after 1 second
- Color-coded by trick type:
  - Flip: Green
  - Grab: Blue
  - Grind: Yellow
  - Special: Gold

**Balance Meter (Bottom Center)**
- Only visible during grinds/manuals
- Analog meter that sways
- Red zones at edges (bail imminent)
- Arrow shows current position

**Special Meter (Bottom Right)**
- Vertical bar that fills up
- Glows when full
- "SPECIAL READY" text appears
- Drains slowly when not tricking

## 12. Loading Screens

**Design:**
- Corporate loading bar that fills (styled like progress bar in project management software)
- Random tips displayed at bottom
- Small animated Tony doing idle tricks in corner
- Level preview image (blurred)

**Tips Pool:**

- "Grinds charge your special meter fastest."
- "Derek keeps his passwords on sticky notes. Just like his morals."
- "Press REVERT immediately when landing to start a manual."
- "Higher combos mean bigger bonuses. Risk vs reward, baby."

## 13. Accessibility

### Visual
- High contrast mode (black/white/yellow only)
- Colorblind modes (Deuteranopia, Protanopia, Tritanopia)
- Scalable UI (75% - 150%)
- Reduce motion (minimize flashing, slow transitions)

### Audio
- Subtitles with speaker labels
- Visual cues for audio events (trick sounds â†’ screen flash)
- Mono audio option

### Input
- Fully remappable controls
- One-handed mode (mobile)
- Hold vs toggle options for grinds/grabs
- Difficulty-based timing windows

## 14. Animation Specifications

### Menu Transitions
- Slide in from direction of navigation
- Duration: 200ms
- Easing: cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)
