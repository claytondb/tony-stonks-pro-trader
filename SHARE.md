You play as Tony Stonks — a hedge fund manager who just got raided by the SEC.

Your only way out? An office chair. On wheels. Through the building.

**Tony Stonks Pro Trader** is a browser-based 3D skating game where you:
- 🪑 Grind desk rails and stair banisters on your office chair
- 🚔 Escape SEC agents across 9 levels (office → stairwell → lobby → highway → forest → train yard → rooftops)
- 📈 Build combos to earn "STONKS" (points) and trigger the cha-ching sound
- 🚀 Land big air tricks off overturned filing cabinets and conference tables
- 💎 Diamond hands your way to freedom

It's THPS meets The Wolf of Wall Street. Built in Three.js + Rapier physics.

Controls: WASD to move, SPACE to ollie, arrow keys for tricks, Q/E to spin.

## 🚀 Product Hunt

**Tagline:** Tony Hawk Pro Skater, but you're escaping the SEC on an office chair

**Description:**
Tony Stonks Pro Trader is a free browser-based 3D skating game with a simple premise: you're a hedge fund manager who just got raided by the SEC, and your only escape is the office chair you're sitting on.

Grind desk rails. Land flip tricks off overturned filing cabinets. Build combo multipliers to earn "STONKS." Escape through 9 escalating levels — office → stairwell → lobby → highway → forest → train yard → rooftops — all the way to a helicopter getaway.

**Features:**
- Full Tony Hawk-style trick system (flips, grabs, grinds, manuals, spins)
- 9 story levels with punchy SEC-vs-trader dialogue
- Procedural sound effects including the cha-ching on every combo land
- Speed stock chart HUD that looks like your portfolio in real time
- NPC officers that actually chase you
- Free to play, no install, runs in any modern browser

Built with Three.js, Rapier physics, and TypeScript. 100% browser-native, no Unity, no download.

**Why we built it:** Because market manipulation is wrong, but *simulated* market manipulation on an office chair is extremely funny.

**Makers:** David Clayton & Nero (AI collaborator)
